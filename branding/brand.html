Logo

Symbolism

The logo combines an upward growth arrow with an infinity-like switch symbol, representing financial growth powered by intelligent switching. The gradient from blue to emerald represents trust to growth.

Usage Guidelines

Always use on dark navy background. Minimum clear space equals the height of the 'F' character. Never distort, recolor, or add effects.

Component Guidelines

Buttons

12px horizontal padding, 6px internal. Rounded (8px). Primary: Solid blue. Secondary: Glass outline. Ghost: No background.

Cards

16px-24px padding. Glass background with 1px stroke at 6% white. 12px-16px border radius. Subtle shadow.

Inputs

Dark surface, 1px white/8% border on focus. 12px padding, 12px radius. Placeholder in gray-500.

Tags & Badges

Pill shape. Gradient background (blue/green) at 15% opacity. 6px 14px padding. 13px font.

Loading States

Shimmer animation on glass cards. Skeleton blocks matching content shape. 60fps, 1.5s cycle.

Charts

Deep navy background. Use primary blue for lines, emerald for fills. Grid lines at 8% white. Crosshair with glow.

Mood Board

Visual Direction

Dark, minimal, spacious. Every element breathes. Content is king — UI disappears when not needed. Data visualizations are works of art. Gradients are subtle but purposeful.

Animation Philosophy

60fps, 300ms standard transitions. Spring animations for interactive elements. Fade + scale for modals. Slide for page transitions. Micro-interactions on every tappable element.
